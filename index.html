What is Nujii Studio?

garageband + googledocs

  • Record and mix in real-time together
  • Share their music instantly
  • Follow their friends

How does it work?

Nujii Studio is an online Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) that uses servers to...

  • Connect users
  • Process audio
  • Store user data
  • Allow users to connect is real-time

Why Nujii Studio?

Because of...

  • Collaborating is slow and difficult
  • Expensive hardware
  • Expensive software
  • Difficult to learn

There are many barriers to entry for young artists

Problems with Current Solutions

Some of the most common problems that current solutions have are...

  • Constant uploading/downloading
  • Formatting issues
  • Slow
  • Limited processing ability

The Solutions We Provide

Via...

  • Real-time collaboration
  • Server does the heavy lifting
  • Operate on SaaS model (cheap)
  • Simple, intuitive design

We are removing the barriers to entry that many young and aspiring musicians face when starting to record their music

Value Proposition

A simpler, more collaborative recording experience

  • Users will be able to work in real-time with their friends
  • Users will be able to connect and find people to help them with their projects
  • Users will have access to plug-in packages that they didn't before
  • Beginners will have access to recording software that they didn't before

The Market

Our target market is the Indie music scene

  • Gallup polls estimate that 52% of US households have active musicians in the US alone
  • In Europe (UK excluded) this number is even higher
  • This number does not acount for the rapidly growing number of electronic musicians (i.e. DJs, Techno, etc.)
  • The fastest growing segment of musicians are ages 18 - 34
  • Globally, the musical instruments and products industry is a $17 billion industry (according to National Association of Music Merchants)

Customer Segments

  • Amateur Musicians
    • Musicians who don't earn a living, but want to share with friends. This would be the majority of the "Indie" scene.
  • Serious Musicians
    • Self promote in effort to earn a living.
  • Casual Recorders
    • Those who don't self promote and just record for fun

Customer Channel

While Nujii has a landing page, the app is distributed through existing channels.

  • Apple App store
  • Android store
  • Microsoft App store (upcoming)
  • Linux store

However, users will be able to access their Nujii Connect account via either the landing page or through the app itself
