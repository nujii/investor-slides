The World is Changing

Independent music is taking the world by storm


  • Gallup polls estimate the number of musicians close to 80,000,000 in the US alone

  • More and more people are beginning to make music, often times with just their computers

  • Nujii Studio aims to change the way these musicians interact...

Customer Segments

18-35 year olds are the fastest growing demographic of musicians

  • Amateur Musicians
    • Musicians who don't earn a living, but want to share with friends.
  • Serious Musicians
    • Self promote in effort to earn a living.
  • Casual Recorders
    • Those who don't self promote and just record for fun

Customer Channel

While Nujii has a landing page, the app is distributed through existing channels.

  • Apple App store
  • Android store
  • Microsoft App store (upcoming)
  • Linux store

However, users will be able to access their Nujii Connect account via either the landing page or through the app itself

A Paradigm Shift...

We envision a world where musicians record and mix their music together in real-time


  • No more sending songs track by track via email

  • No more format problems between platforms

  • No more paying hundreds of dollars for software

  • No more struggling to find people to record with

Nujii Studio

Nujii Studio is better way for musicians to interact

  • Real-time collaboration
  • No formating issues
  • Low cost
  • Instant sharing

Value Proposition

A simpler, more collaborative recording experience

  • Users will be able to work in real-time with their friends
  • Users will be able to connect and find people to help them with their projects
  • Users will have access to plug-in packages that they didn't before
  • Beginners will have access to recording software that they didn't before

The How it works

by pushing the user's data to a server, and then pushing it from there to the collaborator's computers we are able to acheive real-time collaboration
